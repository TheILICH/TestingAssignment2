Test Case: Buy Product Test on DemoBlaze

Purpose: Automate the process of logging in, adding the first product to the cart, and completing a purchase.

Steps:

1. Open Chrome and go to DemoBlaze.

2. Log in using credentials from a Python file.

3. Select the first product (Samsung galaxy s6) using JavaScript.

4. Add the product to the cart, handle the browser alert.

5. Go to the home page, then open the cart.

6. Click "Place Order" and fill in the required fields(only Name and Credit card fields are required).

7. Confirm purchase by submitting the form.

8. Close browser.
